use <span class="hljs-keyword">.border</span> to add 1px solid border with default gray color.

```html
<div class="border h-10">...</div>
```

<div class="border center-center h-10">Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit, sed do eiusmod tempor incididunt ut labore et dolore magna aliqua.</div>

or use <span class="hljs-keyword">.border-{color}</span> for colored border.

```html
<div class="border-red-dark h-10">...</div>
```

<div class="border-red-dark center-center h-10">Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit, sed do eiusmod tempor incididunt ut labore et dolore magna aliqua.</div>
